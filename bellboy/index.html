About us

Bellboy has been created to develop opportunities for job seekers affected by corona or other reasons, taking care of delivery or any specific task for household or any other customer requirements. Our platform provides personal assistance and delivery services for all your needs, while you stay at home with peace of mind our bellboys will take care of the rest following safety SOPs in these challenging times.

Hire-a-Bellboy

Filling the gap in daily household and outdoor needs, through a mobile application we offer a personal assistant service for users to get deliveries of groceries, medicines, food, fruits & vegetables from the preferred store or nearby shop to your door step. It also offers a unique feature to hire-a-bellboy as your personal assistant for a certain period of time, to do multiple or specific outdoor jobs , for example, research of any product (sanitary market prices & latest pictures or school’s visits to collect prospectus), get my laundry, parcel/gift delivery, exchange from a certain store etc. Let our bellboys take care of your needs. In these difficult COVID-19 pandemic times, we are focused on your safety and wellbeing by providing you with our unique service so that you can stay safe at home and all your needs are taken care off. Our versatile bellboys are trained in all safety SOPs ready to serve you at any given time. Bellboy – Always there to serve.
